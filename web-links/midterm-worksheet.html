1. Develop your idea

The challenge I'd like to tackle is to create a website that will allow me to organize and present my work to the general public, including potential employers and other artists. I'd also like the website to highlight my UX design skills, such as good organization, typography, aesthetics, and overall pleasant user experience.

2. Discovery and Research

I researched the number of online portfolios that was established by various artist. I have discovered that they developed website aesthetics that corresponded to their art style in order to offer a consistent theme throughout their portfolio and to represent themselves in a particular manner. Some of them even incorporated interactive entertaining aspects to their websites in order to make them stand out and demonstrate their capabilities in UX design.
